New Pain Point

?
📌 How to use this — for Mariam Step 1 — Find complaints: Go to Facebook groups like "Seven Figure Medicare Agent" or "Independent Insurance Agents". Search for posts where people complain about lead quality, transfer services, ad agencies overpromising, or vendors ghosting them.

Step 2 — Screenshot it: Take a screenshot of the comment or post. You don't need to blur names — AI focuses on the pain, not the person.

Step 3 — Drop it here: Drag the screenshot into the upload zone. Add a quick title like "agent complaining about transfer quality" and hit Generate.

Why we do this: Real complaints = real pain. When The Lead Boss publishes content that directly addresses what agents are already frustrated about, it reads as insider knowledge — not a sales pitch. This is how you build trust at scale.
